Hi, my name is Edoardo and I’m a Data Analyst. I recently graduated from Suffolk University with a Bachelors of Science (Business Analytics and Information Systems).

Trained in Data Science and statistics, I possess an extensive background working with large and varied datasets using advanced statistical methods, data analysis, database management, visualization tools, business intelligence and results communication in order to help companies and researchers analyse their datasets and facilitate decision making.
